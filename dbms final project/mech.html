VISION OF THE DEPARTMENT:

Our vision is to cultivate a dynamic learning environment that empowers students with a deep understanding of fundamental principles, hands-on skills, and a forward-thinking mindset. We aim to instill in our students the passion for continuous learning, adaptability to emerging technologies, and a strong ethical foundation. By promoting interdisciplinary collaboration and engaging in cutting-edge research, we strive to be at the forefront of breakthroughs that shape the future of mechanical engineering.

RESEARCH TRUST AREAS

Advanced Materials and Manufacturing:

Research in the development of new materials with enhanced properties. Exploration of advanced manufacturing techniques, such as additive manufacturing or 3D printing. Investigation into sustainable and eco-friendly materials and processes.

Robotics and Automation:

Development of intelligent robotic systems for industrial, medical, and service applications. Research in autonomous systems and collaborative robotics. Human-robot interaction and safety in robotic environments.

Biomechanics and Medical Devices:

Research in the design and optimization of medical devices. Study of biomechanics for applications in orthopedics, prosthetics, and rehabilitation. Development of assistive technologies for individuals with disabilities

Energy Systems and Renewable Energy:

Study of sustainable energy sources and technologies. Optimization of energy systems for efficiency and environmental impact. Development of advanced energy storage solutions.
