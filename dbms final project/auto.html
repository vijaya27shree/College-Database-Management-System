VISION OF THE DEPARTMENT:

Our vision is to cultivate a learning environment that fosters curiosity, critical thinking, and hands-on experience. We aim to empower students with a deep understanding of automotive engineering principles, cutting-edge technologies, and a commitment to ethical and sustainable practices. By integrating theory with practical applications, we seek to produce graduates who are not only proficient in automotive design and technology but also equipped with the skills to navigate the rapidly evolving landscape of the automotive industry.

RESEARCH TRUST AREAS

Electric and Hybrid Vehicles:

Development of advanced electric and hybrid powertrains. Optimization of battery technologies for improved energy density and charging efficiency. Integration of renewable energy sources in vehicle charging systems.

Autonomous and Connected Vehicles:

Research in autonomous vehicle technology for enhanced safety and efficiency. Development of communication protocols for connected vehicle networks. Human-machine interaction studies for autonomous and semi-autonomous systems.

Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS):

Improvement of ADAS technologies for enhanced safety features. Research in real-time sensor fusion for more accurate and reliable detection. Integration of machine learning and artificial intelligence for predictive analysis.

Energy Systems and Renewable Energy:

Study of sustainable energy sources and technologies. Optimization of energy systems for efficiency and environmental impact. Development of advanced energy storage solutions.
