FACILITIES


Automotive Engine Testing Lab Conduct experiments related to the performance and efficiency of internal combustion engines. Experiments: Engine mapping, fuel efficiency tests, emission testing, and combustion analysis.

Vehicle Dynamics Lab Study the behavior of vehicles under different conditions to optimize handling, stability, and safety. Experiments: Tire testing, suspension analysis, and vehicle dynamics simulations.

Automotive Electronics Lab Explore electronic systems used in modern vehicles. Experiments: Study engine control units (ECUs), electronic stability control, and communication networks in vehicles.

Vehicle Design and Prototyping Lab Engage in the conceptualization and prototyping of vehicle components and systems. Activities: 3D modeling, design optimization, and physical prototyping.
