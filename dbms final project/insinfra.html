FACILITIES


Control Systems Lab: This lab is dedicated to the study of control systems and the design of controllers. Students work with different types of control systems, such as proportional-integral-derivative (PID) controllers, and study their behavior in real-time. Experiments may involve the control of temperature, pressure, and level using feedback control systems.

Process Control Lab: This lab is dedicated to studying advanced control systems used in industrial processes. Students work with pilot-scale process plants and implement control strategies for processes like temperature control, flow control, and pressure control.

Instrumentation Design Lab: This lab involves designing and building instruments for specific applications. Students may work on projects such as designing a data acquisition system, building a signal conditioning circuit, or developing a custom instrument for a specific purpose.

Microprocessor and Microcontroller Lab: This lab focuses on programming and interfacing microprocessors and microcontrollers. Students learn to design and implement control algorithms using microcontrollers, which are essential in many instrumentation and control applications.
