VISION OF THE DEPARTMENT:

The vision of the Production Technology Department at our college is to cultivate a dynamic learning environment that seamlessly integrates theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience. Our aim is to empower students with cutting-edge skills in manufacturing, automation, and quality control, preparing them for the rapidly evolving industrial landscape. Through innovative curriculum design and collaboration with industry leaders, we envision fostering a community of forward-thinking engineers who can contribute to technological advancements and address real-world challenges. The department strives to be a hub of creativity, research, and practical application, equipping students with the expertise to excel in diverse sectors of production technology.

RESEARCH TRUST AREAS

MECHATRONICS:

Research in mechatronics involves the interdisciplinary study and integration of mechanical engineering, electronics, computer science, and control engineering to design and create intelligent systems and products.

BIOMANUFACTURING:

Research in biomanufacturing focuses on the application of biological systems and processes for the production of valuable materials, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, and bio-based products

ADDITIVE MANUFACTURING:

Research in additive engineering, commonly known as additive manufacturing or 3D printing, explores innovative methods of creating objects layer by layer from digital models. This field encompasses a variety of materials, including plastics, metals, ceramics, and biomaterials.

SUSTAINABLE MANUFACTURING:

Research in sustainable manufacturing is dedicated to developing environmentally friendly and socially responsible practices within the manufacturing industry. This interdisciplinary field focuses on minimizing the environmental impact of manufacturing processes, reducing resource consumption, and promoting social well-being.
