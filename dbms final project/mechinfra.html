FACILITIES


Thermodynamics Lab Conduct experiments related to the principles of thermodynamics. Experiments: Heat transfer studies, analysis of thermodynamic cycles, and testing of heat exchangers.

Hydraulics and Pneumatics Lab Study fluid power systems and their applications. Experiments: Hydraulic and pneumatic system design, analysis of components.

Metrology and Quality Control Lab Focus on precision measurements and quality control. Experiments: Measurement of geometrical parameters, calibration techniques.

Automotive Engineering Lab Provide insights into automotive systems and technologies. Experiments: Engine performance analysis, vehicle dynamics studies, and emission testing.
