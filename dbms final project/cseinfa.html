FACILITIES


The Distributed Computing Lab at Computer Science Department serves as a dynamic hub for exploration, experimentation, and innovation in the realm of distributed systems. This cutting-edge facility is designed to provide students and researchers with hands-on experience and practical insights into the complexities of distributed computing, a field critical to modern computing architectures.

The Programming Practice Lab is a cornerstone of the computer science curriculum, instilling in students the practical skills and problem-solving mindset essential for success in the field of software development. Through its emphasis on hands-on learning, collaboration, and industry relevance, the lab plays a vital role in shaping well-rounded and proficient programmers.

The Internet of Things (IoT) Lab at Computer Science Department is a specialized facility dedicated to exploring, experimenting, and innovating in the rapidly evolving field of IoT. This lab is designed to provide students and researchers with hands-on experience in developing, testing, and deploying IoT solutions.

The Data Science Lab at Computer Science Department is a dynamic facility dedicated to advancing research, education, and innovation in the field of data science. Equipped with cutting-edge technologies and tools, this lab serves as a hub for students and researchers to explore, analyze, and derive insights from complex datasets.
